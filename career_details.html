Business Development Manager, Platform Solutions

at G-Tech Ventures (View all jobs)
Berlin, Germany

The Opportunity:

We are seeking an account executive to join the Platform Solutions team. This team builds and grows strategic relationships with high-value technology companies, enabling access to Shutterstock content directly within external applications. Through the G-Tech API, we power integrations with companies such as Facebook, Google, Microsoft, and Pinterest.

As an account executive, you will develop new revenue-generating partnerships with top consumer and enterprise tech companies. These are product-driven partnerships which require a strategic, consultative business development approach. The ideal candidate will be able to hunt for qualified opportunities, propose innovative solutions that create long-term value for our partners and effectively negotiate and close deals. This is an mid-level position with high earning potential based on performance.
